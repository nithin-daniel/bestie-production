our story

give best service to our customers
Bestie was born out of a deep passion for showcasing the natural splendor and cultural richness of Wayanad to the world. We believe that every traveler deserves a haven that not only provides comfort but also immerses them in the local culture and surroundings. With this vision, we embarked on a journey to create a collection of unique resorts and hotels that truly capture the essence of Wayanad.

Your Wayanad Adventure Awaits

Whether you're an intrepid explorer or a leisure seeker, Wayanad has something to offer everyone, and Bestie is your trusted companion on this journey. Come, immerse yourself in the magic of Wayanad, and let us be your best friend on this unforgettable adventure.

At Bestie, we are driven by a philosophy that transcends traditional hospitality. We believe in creating not just a place to rest but a space where moments turn into memories, and guests become a part of our extended family. Our motto is "Your Wayanad, Your Way," and we are dedicated to helping you craft your ideal Wayanad experience, whether it involves relaxation, adventure, cultural exploration, or a bit of everything.
